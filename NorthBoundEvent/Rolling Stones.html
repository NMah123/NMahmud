Rolling stones
The No Filter Tour is a Rolling Stones European/North American concert tour that began on September 9, 2017 in Hamburg, Germany. The trip was supposed to end in 2020, but owing to the COVID-19 epidemic, it had to be postponed. The trip will resume in September 2021, according to the announcement. The Stones announced a few weeks later that drummer Charlie Watts had undergone an unspecified medical procedure and would be unable to join the tour due to a lengthy recovery period. Watts passed away on August 24, 2021. Steve Jordan, a long-time Stones colleague, will stand in as drummer for the 2021 performances.
